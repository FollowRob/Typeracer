How to Use the Typing Speed Test
  1. Select your desired difficulty level from the dropdown menu.
  2. Read the sample text provided.
  3. Click in the typing box and begin typing the sample text. The timer will start automatically when you begin typing.
  4. As you type, correct words will be highlighted in blue and incorrect words in red.
  5. When you finish, press the Enter key to stop the timer and see your results.
  6. Your time, words per minute (WPM), and chosen difficulty will be displayed in the results area.
  7. Click Retry to try again with a new sample text.
